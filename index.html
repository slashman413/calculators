FAQ

How is BMI calculated?

BMI = weight in kilograms divided by height in metres squared. It's a rough screening figure, not a medical diagnosis.

Does the loan calculator work for any currency?

Yes — it computes the payment from the amount, interest rate and term regardless of currency.

Are my inputs saved?

No — every calculation runs locally and nothing is stored.
